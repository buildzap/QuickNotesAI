Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

At QuickNotesAI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our task management platform.

Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
